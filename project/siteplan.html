Site Plan

Site Name: Axis

Site's Purpose

This Website promotes a local starting company in Durango, Mexico, that specializes in the manufacturing and distribution of fashinable clothing that keeps up with modern trends, and also caters to the needs of several businesses by providing uniforms, designs, etc.

Scenarios

Some questions asked by visitors that this website should answer:

Where can I get trendy clothing in Durango at a fair price? Can I order custom clothing for my business? What are the latest fashion trends available at Axis? Can I order clothing online? What makes this business different from others?
